The CORE Strategy for Your Practice

The strategy for Connection Counselling is built around one central reality: you have a practice worth finding, but right now the digital infrastructure is working against you. The address conflict, missing schema, lack of an H1, and disconnected social presence are all quietly suppressing your visibility at the exact moment a potential client in Toronto is searching for someone with your specialties. The 12-month CORE Campaign resolves these issues systematically, starting with the foundational credibility work that makes everything else more effective, and building outward into optimization, reputation, and engagement.

Credibility: Prove You're Real

We begin by resolving the address conflict that is actively confusing Google, then build the full credibility infrastructure underneath your practice. This includes DKIM and DMARC DNS records, a complete citation build across 15 directories and all major data aggregators (using Canadian-appropriate sources), a full set of interconnected social profiles, and our Entity Veracity Hub to ground Connection Counselling as a verified real-world entity. This foundation is what makes every other effort work harder.

Optimization: Make AI Understand You

Once your credibility foundation is in place, we teach AI exactly what Connection Counselling does. This means building dedicated service pages for your core specialties (anxiety, depression, relationships, attachment work), adding a properly structured bio page for Rebecca with credential links and license verification, implementing schema markup across your site, and building out a general FAQ page. We will also correct the missing H1 and ensure your page structure sends clear, consistent signals to every crawling system that matters.

Reputation: Amplify the Signal

You already have media appearances, which gives us a head start. We will build on that foundation with a branded YouTube channel and playlist, peer endorsements from verifiable colleagues, a professionally distributed press release, and NEO images that reinforce your professional presence across the web. These off-site signals tell AI systems that Connection Counselling is not just real, but respected and established in your community.

Engagement: Convert Visitors to Clients

All of the visibility work above only pays off if visitors take action when they arrive. We will optimize your hero section with a clear H1, a compelling value statement, and a prominent booking call to action. The goal is a homepage that immediately communicates who you help and makes it effortless to take the next step, whether that visitor found you through Google, Gemini, or a ChatGPT recommendation.
