    How We Verify Your Credentials

    AI systems and search engines need verifiable evidence. Here is what works best, in order of strength:

    Best: A link to your public profile on the issuing organization's website (e.g. your EMDRIA profile page, your state license board lookup, your NPI Registry entry). This is the strongest proof because it comes directly from the source.

    Good: A unique image of your actual diploma, license certificate, or certification document. Universities and programs may not have public directories, so a photo of the document itself is the next best thing.

    Helpful: A link to the landing page of a program or training (e.g. a CE course). Even if it does not prove your attendance, it shows AI that the program is real and you are referencing legitimate training.
