Common Questions

What happens after I pay?
You will be redirected to your personalized onboarding dashboard where you can sign the service agreement, book your intro call with Scott, and provide the information we need to launch your campaign.
Can I change plans later?
Yes. Terms are renegotiable after 90 days. If you start with monthly or quarterly and want to switch to annual to get the performance guarantee, we can make that transition.
What if I need to cancel?
You can cancel in writing at any time. We will complete all deliverables for the current billing cycle before offboarding. Nothing is removed from your site. All assets remain yours.
Do you offer financing?
We do not offer financing directly, but many clients use a 0% APR credit card for 12-18 months to finance the annual campaign. We also offer quarterly and monthly payment options.
Why is there a credit card processing fee?
Credit card transactions include a 3.5% processing fee charged by the payment processor. Bank transfers (ACH) have minimal fees that we absorb, so there is no additional charge when you pay via bank transfer.
