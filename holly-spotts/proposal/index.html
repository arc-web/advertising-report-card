The CORE Strategy for Your Practice

Every element of the CORE Marketing System addresses a specific gap we found in Full Cup Wellness's current digital presence. Rather than patching one or two things, we build the complete foundation that Google and AI require - starting with proving you exist, then teaching AI exactly who you help, then amplifying your 10 years of expertise through reputation signals, and finally ensuring every visitor who finds you has a clear and easy path to booking a session.

Credibility: Prove You're Real

We start by grounding Full Cup Wellness in the real world. This means setting up DNS trust records (DKIM and DMARC) on your domain, building out 15+ directory citations plus all five major data aggregators so your practice name, address, and phone number are consistent everywhere AI looks. We also build and interconnect your social profiles - YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and more - using our Rising Tide strategy so each platform points back to the others and to your website. For an online-only practice like yours, this interconnected footprint is the difference between being trusted and being ignored.

Optimization: Make AI Understand You

Next, we make it impossible for AI to misunderstand what you do. We build five dedicated service pages around your most important specialties - stress and burnout for professionals, anxiety, codependency, and therapy for physicians - each with proper heading structure, schema markup, and FAQ sections based on what your ideal clients are actually searching. We also build a comprehensive bio page for you as Dr. Holly Spotts, Psy.D, with your credentials, license verification links, NPI number, education, and clinical approach all in a format that AI can read, trust, and cite. A general FAQ page covering your private-pay model, telehealth format, and getting-started process rounds out the optimization layer.

Reputation: Amplify the Signal

Your 10 years of experience and Psy.D are genuine differentiators - we make them visible. We create and optimize your YouTube channel and produce short-form video content that demonstrates your expertise to both potential clients and AI systems. We distribute press releases through professional networks to create third-party mentions of Full Cup Wellness, and we capture peer endorsements and professional association memberships in a format that adds verifiable authority to your bio page and schema. This is how we take the trust that currently lives in your referral network and broadcast it digitally.

Engagement: Convert Visitors to Clients

Finally, we make sure the visitors who find you actually book. We optimize your homepage hero section so that a high-achieving, overwhelmed woman lands on your site and immediately recognizes herself in your words. We sharpen your call-to-action so the path from "I need help" to "I've booked a session" is frictionless. With your telehealth practice open to all of California and no geographic barrier to entry, even a modest improvement in traffic driven by the first three pillars can translate directly into consistent new client inquiries every week.

You shared that your average session rate is $200 and your current caseload sits at 10 clients per week.
