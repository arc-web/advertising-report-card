Anonymized findings from an actual therapy practice entity audit

Google does not recognize this practice as an entity. Knowledge Graph score is 0/100. No Google AI Overview is generated for the brand name. This means AI search literally cannot recommend the practice, even though the content is excellent.

Schema stack is strong but incomplete. 8 schema types detected (0.72/1.0), but FAQPage, VideoObject, and AggregateRating are missing. These three additions alone are the most impactful changes available.

Already dominant in conversational AI. Rank #1 in both Claude and Perplexity with a CRES score of 100/100. Content authenticity scores a perfect 5/5. The foundation is strong, the gaps are structural.
