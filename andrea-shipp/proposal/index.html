The CORE Strategy for Your Practice

Here is how we will transform your digital presence across all four CORE pillars in three months, building the foundation for sustainable growth that does not depend on any single referral source.

C - Credibility: Prove You Exist

We will build your verified entity footprint from the ground up. This includes setting up DNS authentication records, building out 15+ directory citations plus all 5 major data aggregators, creating your Rising Tide social profiles (YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, and more), and launching your Entity Veracity Hub to connect cryptographic data points that prove your practice is real and operating.

O - Optimization: Teach AI About Your Services

We will create 5 dedicated target pages for your key specialties - first responder counseling, EMDR therapy, trauma therapy, anxiety counseling, and couples therapy. Each page gets custom HTML, proper heading hierarchy, schema markup, and FAQ sections based on what real people are asking Google. We will also optimize your existing pages and bio to be AI-readable.

R - Reputation: Amplify Your Expertise

We will help you collect professional endorsements from colleagues and supervisors, then amplify them across GBP, social media, YouTube, and Quora. We will syndicate a press release across 500+ news sites, and distribute NEO images (AI-generated, practice-branded visuals) to high-authority platforms as a modern form of backlinking.

E - Engagement: Guide Visitors to Book

We will redesign your hero sections with emotionally compelling language that speaks directly to first responders and their families, clear service explanations, and direct calls-to-action that reduce friction between "I found this therapist" and "I booked a consultation." Every page becomes a clear path to your calendar.
